Wow Thai Bistro

A new Thai spot has made its debut in the neighborhood. Called , the new arrival is located at 701 Randolph St. (between Ralston St. & 19th Ave.) in Ingleside Heights.

Wow Thai Bistro took over from the former April Nine Thai Kitchen, but has kept its menu relatively similar. Expect to see items like baby back ribs with Thai-style fried rice, noodle dishes like pad thai and pad see ew, grilled items like garlic chicken and crispy pork belly, salads, and more.

Wow Thai Bistro has a 3.5 stars on Yelp and 4 stars on Google.
